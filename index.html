About Me

Junior full-stack developer from Sheffield, UK.

After an 8 year career in teaching and childcare, I began studying at the Open University, achieving a distinction for my Certificate of Higher Education in Computing, IT and Design.

Following the completion of my qualification, I spent 3 months travelling around Europe with my partner, returning to Sheffield in late 2022 to start studying with The Developer Academy in early 2023. While working with TDA, I learned the fundamentals of full-stack web development and became familiar with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, Node.js and React.

Beyond programming, I have a deep love for video games, which fuels my curiosity and enthusiasm for interactive digital experiences.

Additionally, I'm a passionate musician and enjoy playing guitar and piano, and creating my own video game music.
